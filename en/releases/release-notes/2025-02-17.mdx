This update marks a major milestone with the launch of both Pro and Free plans, making Giselle accessible to everyone.

## Pro Plan Launch

The **Pro plan** is now available with time-based pricing for AI agent usage. Access premium features and higher usage limits with flexible subscription options.

Learn more: [Introducing Time-Based Pricing: Giselle's Approach to AI Agents](https://giselles.ai/blog/time-based-pricing-ai-agents)

## Free Plan Launch

**Free plan** released, making Giselle accessible to everyone. Start building AI workflows at no cost with generous usage limits.

## Monorepo Migration

Migrated to a **monorepo architecture** for better code organization and development workflow.
